üî¨ What is Physics?

Physics is the fundamental science that seeks to understand how the universe works. From the tiniest subatomic particles to the vast expanses of galaxies, physics provides the framework for comprehending matter, energy, space, and time.

It is the foundation upon which all natural sciences are built, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications that shape our modern world.
